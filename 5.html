What is Taste?

Philosophy tries to explore all the ways taste can be defined.

Taste can be objective:

  • Sweet
  • Salt
  • Sour
  • Bitter
  • Savory

Taste can be subjective:

  • "What a beatiful island"
  • "I like tall women"
  • "I love classic music"
  • "The beauty of poetry.
